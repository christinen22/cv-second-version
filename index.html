Who is Christine?

I am a lively, curious and creative soul who chose to change career paths as I did not feel satisfied in my current position. For about 10 years I have worked in accounting and administration and then used my free time to give vent to my creativity. At the age of 35, I decided to look for the exact profession where I can combine my strength in problem solving and IT with my great interest in creativity. I landed here, at the Media Institute and as a future front end developer, and I'm having a blast!

A little personal stuff about me; I am 36 years old and originally from Malmö but have been living in Ystad for a year now. I always have creative projects going on, it can be anything from a graphic logo, to knitting (there are usually several projects going on at the same time...), sewing, general "crafts". I also enjoy being in the kitchen, where I both bake and cook.
